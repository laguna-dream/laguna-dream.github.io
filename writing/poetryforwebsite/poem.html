NaPoWriMo 2026

16th April 2026

Today, try writing a poem in which you describe something that cannot speak, and what it has taught or told you.

My sturdy sweet little machine(s)
How might I ever tell you
How much delight you've brought me!

You've hosted me well
as I've clung to the engine of a plane,
hurled myself across
continents, rolling across a 1000 beds at once,
bringing home all sorts of strange suspicious creatures,
that you've made room for, let them run

Sometimes i don't listen to you, oh -
i have not been the best and don't i know it
i tried, i really did, we had our little honeymoon
phase didn't we
i held you tender, swathed you up in the softest threads
patted you while your engines sped

You let me do what I want to do. Mostly. Sometimes we have little tiffs
and i ponder
is this what i really want?
but i find my little ways,
we make do

i've tried to learn your ways,
speak to you in what feels like ancient rumblings
whistles and coos

but sometimes it feels like
all i need to do is just place my hand on you

my sturdy sweet little machine(s)!
there is yet so much to see,
so much to do!
i am so glad i can do it with you
